# Pensive Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 14, 2026**

Pensive is a personal finance app for recording and organizing expenses, income, recurring items, reimbursements, and notes. This policy explains what information Pensive handles and the choices available to you.

## Information you provide

When you create and use a Pensive account, you may provide:

- A username and password for account access.
- Expenses, income, dates, amounts, accounts, categories, payback details, recurring-item details, and notes.
- Notepad content, tables, and preferences such as custom categories and colors.

Pensive does not ask for access to your contacts, photos, camera, location, health data, or advertising identifier.

## How information is used

Pensive uses this information only to provide and improve the app's core features, including synchronizing your data between the web and iPhone apps, displaying summaries and tracking views, maintaining your account session, and responding to support requests.

Pensive does not sell your personal information and does not use your financial information for advertising. Pensive does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

## Where information is stored

Your account and app data are stored in a Convex-managed cloud database and processed by the service providers needed to operate Pensive. Data is associated with your account and access is restricted by authenticated user ownership in the application.

Pensive is **not end-to-end encrypted**. User-entered financial and note data is stored in the database in readable application form rather than being encrypted with a key that only you control. Please do not enter information you would not want stored on a cloud service.

Passwords are handled by the authentication system and are not stored by Pensive as readable plaintext passwords. On iPhone, authentication tokens are stored in the system Keychain. Some display and filter preferences may be stored locally on your device or in your browser.

## Sharing and service providers

Pensive does not share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers. Information may be processed by infrastructure and hosting providers, including Convex, solely as needed to provide, secure, and maintain the service. Pensive may disclose information when reasonably necessary to comply with law, protect the service, or prevent fraud and abuse.

## Retention and deletion

Your information is retained while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account from the account controls in Pensive. Account deletion is designed to remove your account, authentication records, and associated expenses, income, recurring items, payback links, options, and notepad data from the Pensive database. Some technical records may remain temporarily in provider backups or logs and will be removed according to the provider's normal retention procedures.

## Security

Pensive uses authenticated access controls and reasonable measures intended to protect account and app data. No internet service can guarantee absolute security, and Pensive cannot promise that unauthorized access will never occur.

## Children's privacy

Pensive is not directed to children under 13, and Pensive does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as Pensive changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

## Contact

For privacy questions, account deletion help, or requests concerning your information, contact the Pensive team through the support contact listed in the App Store listing.
